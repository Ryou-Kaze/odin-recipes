Risotto

Description

Risotto, a traditional Italian dish, is renowned for its creamy texture and rich flavor. Made with Arborio rice, it is gently cooked in a savory broth, typically chicken or vegetable, which is added gradually to allow the rice to release its natural starches. This creates a velvety consistency that is further enriched with a splash of white wine, a hint of onion, and a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese. Finished with a pat of butter and seasoned to perfection, risotto is a comforting and indulgent dish that pairs well with a variety of ingredients, from simple herbs to luxurious truffles.

Ingredients

  • Arborio rice - 1 cup (200 grams)
  • Chicken or vegetable broth - 4 cups (1 liter)
  • Onion - 1 small, finely chopped
  • White wine - 1/2 cup (120 ml)
  • Parmesan cheese - 1/2 cup (50 grams), grated
  • Butter - 2 tablespoons
  • Olive oil - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - 1 teaspoon (adjust to taste)
  • Pepper - 1/2 teaspoon (adjust to taste)

Optional but recommended for added flavor:

  • Fresh parsley for garnish - a handful, chopped

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:

    • Finely chop the onion.
    • Grate the Parmesan cheese.

  2. Heat the Broth:

    • In a saucepan, heat 4 cups (1 liter) of chicken or vegetable broth and keep it warm over low heat.

  3. Cook the Aromatics:

    • In a large pan, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat.
    • Add the chopped onion and cook until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.

  4. Toast the Rice:

    • Add 1 cup (200 grams) of Arborio rice to the pan and cook, stirring constantly, for 2-3 minutes until the rice is lightly toasted and coated with the oil and butter.

  5. Add the Wine:

    • Pour in 1/2 cup (120 ml) of white wine and stir until it is mostly absorbed by the rice.

  6. Cook the Risotto:

    • Begin adding the warm broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently.
    • Allow each addition of broth to be absorbed before adding the next.
    • Continue this process until the rice is creamy and cooked through, about 18-20 minutes.

  7. Finish the Risotto:

    • Stir in the remaining 1 tablespoon of butter and 1/2 cup (50 grams) of grated Parmesan cheese.
    • Season with 1 teaspoon of salt and 1/2 teaspoon of pepper, adjusting to taste.

  8. Serve:

    • Serve the risotto hot, optionally garnished with chopped fresh parsley.

Enjoy your classic risotto!
