Pizza

Description

A classic pizza features a thin, homemade crust topped with tangy tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and a drizzle of olive oil. Baked to perfection, it offers a savory and satisfying taste experience with every bite.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour - 2 1/2 cups (300 grams)
  • Warm water - 1 cup (240 ml)
  • Active dry yeast - 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet)
  • Salt - 1 teaspoon
  • Olive oil - 1 tablespoon
  • Tomato sauce - 1/2 cup (120 ml)
  • Mozzarella cheese - 2 cups (200 grams), shredded
  • Olive oil - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon

Optional but recommended for added flavor:

  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Prepare Dough:

    • Mix 2 1/2 cups (300 grams) flour, 1 cup (240 ml) warm water, 2 1/4 tsp yeast, 1 tsp salt, and 1 tbsp olive oil. Knead until smooth, then let rise for 1 hour.

  2. Shape and Top Pizza:

    • Preheat oven to 475-500°F (240-260°C). Divide dough, roll out into thin circles. Place on baking sheet.
    • Spread 1/4 cup (60 ml) tomato sauce on each. Top with 1 cup (100 grams) shredded mozzarella, drizzle with 1/2 tbsp olive oil, sprinkle with salt.

  3. Bake and Serve:

    • Bake 10-12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese melts. Cool briefly, slice, and serve.

Enjoy your classic pizza!
